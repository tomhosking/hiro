The Hotel Navona is centrally located in Rome, providing easy access to major attractions. The breakfast was described as average with small, basic rooms, some of which were noisy and dirty. Staff were consistently described as friendly and helpful, making up for any drawbacks. The hotel is in an old palazzo and retains much of its original charm. Some rooms feature 14th century paintings on the ceilings. Overall, the hotel's prime location and friendly staff make up for its minor drawbacks.
The Rome Marriott Grand Hotel Flora offers beautiful views of the city, Vatican, and Villa Borghese park from its top floor restaurant and rooftop terrace. The hotel is walking distance to major attractions and has helpful and friendly staff. The breakfast buffet is a highlight, though some find it overpriced. Rooms vary in size and view quality. The location is convenient, with numerous restaurants and shops nearby. The service and facilities are of high quality.
The Fairfield Inn and Suites Key West received positive reviews for its friendly and helpful staff, attractive and nice pool area, and free parking. However, some guests were disappointed with the small and shabby rooms, lack of storage space, and noise from parking and adjacent rooms. The continental breakfast was also mentioned as a plus.
The Grand Hotel & Spa offers stunning ocean views from most rooms, with some having a direct view of the boardwalk and beach. The hotel's design ensures that guests can enjoy the ocean despite room location. The beach is clean, and the location is convenient for easy access. Rooms are described as clean and large, but some note the need for improvement in terms of lighting and room condition. Overall, guests had positive experiences and appreciated the ocean access.
The Le Saint-Sulpice is a hotel in Old Montreal offering spacious, comfortable rooms with kitchenettes and great views. Rooms are beautifully appointed with large beds and high-quality linens. The hotel is centrally located near the Notre Dame Cathedral and offers excellent service, including room service, turn-down service, and helpful staff. The restaurant serves excellent food, and the hotel is pet-friendly. The overall experience is described as wonderful, comfortable, and worth the value.
The Hotel Regina Barcelona is centrally located near Plaza Catalunya and Las Ramblas, offering free internet access throughout the hotel. Rooms are clean and comfortable with modern amenities, including large bathrooms with toiletries. The hotel is conveniently located for exploring Barcelona on foot or by public transport, and offers excellent value for travelers. The staff are friendly and helpful, and have accommodated special requests. Overall, the Hotel Regina is highly recommended for its great location, cleanliness, and comfort.
The Hotel America in Barcelona offers a rooftop pool and lounge area with stunning views, as well as a gym and sauna downstairs. The pool is small and not heated, with some reports of it being closed during colder months for repairs. The location is ideal, close to metro stations and various restaurants, but some rooms can be noisy. The staff are described as friendly and helpful, and the hotel is clean and well-maintained. Overall, the Hotel America is a recommended choice for budget-conscious travelers looking for a comfortable base in Barcelona.
The Aldrovandi Villa Borghese is a luxury 5-star hotel in Rome with a reliable and frequent shuttle service to the Spanish Steps. The hotel boasts a beautiful pool and garden area, spacious and luxurious rooms, and friendly and helpful staff. While expensive, guests highly recommend the hotel for its comfort and convenience, with many praising the excellent food and nearby restaurant recommendations.
The AW Cicerone Hotel is located within walking distance to the Vatican, with some reviews praising its excellent location. However, some guests reported issues with rude front desk staff and small, tired rooms. The breakfast received mixed reviews, with some describing it as great and plentiful, while others found it average or boring. Overall, the hotel was considered clean but not luxurious.
The Hotel Colombina in Venice is highly recommended for its friendly and helpful staff, excellent and plentiful breakfasts, and ideal location near St. Mark's Square. The hotel offers a variety of room options with great views and is easily accessible by water taxi. The guests appreciate the courtesy, professionalism, and eagerness of the staff to assist. The overall experience is described as enchanting and worth repeating.
The Mercure Amsterdam Arthur Frommer hotel offers comfortable beds, modern bathrooms, and stylishly decorated rooms in a quiet residential area. Its location is within easy walking distance of major tourist sites, shops, restaurants, and public transportation. The hotel is fully modernized, but some guests found the rooms small and the breakfast expensive. The staff were friendly and helpful, making for a pleasant stay overall. However, some reported issues with noise and dead bugs.
The Giorgione Hotel offers exceptional to ordinary breakfasts, some with a buffet option, and is conveniently located near Rialto Bridge and San Marco Square. Rooms range from small to spacious, with helpful and friendly staff. The hotel is a hidden gem with classic Venetian decor and reasonable value for the price. The food selection at breakfast is plentiful and of high standard, with a mix of hot and cold dishes.
The U232 Hotel is located in an upscale neighborhood, ideally situated for easy access to the metro system, with multiple metro stops within a short walking distance. Rooms are modern and well-appointed, and the hotel offers free high-speed internet. The staff are friendly and helpful, and the location is quiet yet convenient to tourist sites and amenities. Overall, the hotel provides great value for the price.
The Quality Inn & Suites Downtown in New Orleans is highly regarded for its exceptional staff who make guests feel welcome and valued. The hotel offers clean and comfortable rooms, a good free breakfast, and a convenient location close to popular attractions. The customer service is outstanding, with friendly and helpful staff who go above and beyond to ensure a pleasant stay. The hotel is affordable and offers secure parking for an extra fee.
The Hotel Grifo offers small, clean rooms, with some describing them as tiny. The location is excellent, close to major Rome attractions, and the staff are consistently described as friendly, helpful, and accommodating. Breakfast is simple but sufficient for a day of sightseeing. Overall, guests appreciate the value and convenience of the hotel.
The Ca' Pisani Hotel is a modern, art deco design hotel located in a beautiful and unique part of Venice, close to the Academia bridge. The interior design is stunning and contemporary, with attention to detail. Rooms and bathrooms are well-appointed and air-conditioned. The staff are friendly, helpful, and multilingual, providing excellent and knowledgeable service. The hotel offers a free breakfast buffet and free WiFi. The on-site restaurant provides delicious food. Overall, the Ca' Pisani Hotel offers a practical yet beautiful and unique stay in Venice.
The Hotel Adriano in Rome received positive reviews for its excellent and delicious included breakfast, great location with easy access to major attractions, and clean rooms. Some negative comments included issues with older rooms, odors, and noise. Overall, guests appreciated the value and convenience of the hotel.
The River Hotel offers small, clean and quiet rooms in a historic building near Chicago's Magnificent Mile. Some reviews note slow elevators and thin walls, while others praise the affordable prices and convenient location. Room size was a common concern, with some finding them smaller than expected. Staff service was hit-or-miss, and some reported rudeness. Amenities are limited, but the hotel's proximity to shops, restaurants, and tourist attractions makes up for it.
The Murray Hill East Suites offer clean and spacious rooms, some with kitchenettes, in an older building with dated decor. The location is convenient, close to Grand Central Station and public transportation. Prices are reasonable, but some amenities like room service, a hotel cafe or bar, and a business center are lacking. The front desk staff have been reported as unhelpful and rude by some guests.
The City Club Hotel offers comfortable and luxurious rooms with top-notch beds and linens, modern bathrooms with excellent amenities, and friendly and accommodating staff. The hotel's location between 5th and 6th avenues in midtown Manhattan is ideal, providing easy access to Times Square, Bryant Park, and Grand Central Station. Room service is prompt and efficient, and the restaurant on the ground floor is highly-regarded. Some rooms may be smaller than expected, but the overall experience is excellent.
The Hotel Saint Christophe is centrally located in the Latin Quarter, close to multiple Metro stations and attractions such as Notre Dame and Jardin Luxembourg. Rooms are clean and adequate, though small, and breakfast offers a variety of options. The staff are friendly and helpful, making for a pleasant stay. The hotel is conveniently located near shops, restaurants, and markets. The bathrooms are clean and modern, though some may be smaller than expected for Paris.
The Four Seasons Hotel Seattle offers excellent and attentive service, beautiful and delicious food, stunning water views, and a convenient location near popular attractions such as Pike Place Market. Rooms are modern and well-appointed, and the pool area is a popular feature. Some reviews note minor negatives such as small pool size or lack of indoor pool, but overall, guests highly recommend the hotel for a first-class experience.
The Best Western Airport Executive offers a free and efficient shuttle service to and from the airport, as well as a continental breakfast. Rooms are clean and comfortable, but some guests have reported issues with maintenance and handicap accommodations. Breakfast offerings vary from ordinary to outstanding, and some guests found the fitness room and pool disappointing. Hot water issues have been reported in some rooms. The hotel is clean and quiet, but there are limited dining options nearby. Guests appreciated the helpful front desk staff.
The Hotel Erwin in Venice, California, is known for its stunning views of the ocean, beach, and city from the rooftop bar and lounge, HIGH. The hotel offers colorful, eclectically decorated rooms with various views, including ocean and mountain vistas. The location is in the heart of Venice Beach's culture, and the restaurant, LUSH, is highly recommended. While some areas of the hotel and its surroundings may be different from other hotels in the area, the unique experience, excellent views, and delicious food make it a highly recommended stay.
The Roman Spa Hot Springs Resort is known for its beautifully maintained grounds with multiple mineral pools and hot tubs, providing a relaxing atmosphere for guests. The rooms are clean and comfortable, and the resort is conveniently located near restaurants and shops in Calistoga.
